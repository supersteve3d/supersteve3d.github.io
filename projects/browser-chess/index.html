Major feature generations only.

Made Classic the default chess set, kept fun captures on, and tightened game settings into shorter rows.

Scaled capture effects

Made fun capture effects the default and scaled each burst by the captured piece value.

Fun capture mode

Added a colorful custom chess set and playful capture animations with bigger sound effects.

v5.2

Mobile layout and review arrows

Reordered panels for phone play and added tap controls for stepping through move history.

Generous online scoring

Made Mum vs David online games worth much more and clarified that casual same-device local games do not score competition points.

v5.0

Online rooms

Added phone-to-phone Mum vs David games with shared room codes, live board syncing, and saved head-to-head results.

Scoring guide

Competition points

Versioned changelog

Competition dashboard

Added the wider desktop layout, medal scoreboard, saved-game browser, grouped captures, and stable game-history panels.

Shared game archive

Game review tools

Example: winning a Mum vs David online game in 24 moves scores 22 points: 20 base points plus a 2 point quick-win bonus. Beating Hard in 24 moves scores 10 points. Same-device local games are for practice and review, so they do not add competition points. Conservative medals mean tied players share the lower medal unless someone is clearly ahead.
